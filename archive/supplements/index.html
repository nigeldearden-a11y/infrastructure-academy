The Supplement Framework

The four supplements provide a comprehensive framework for engaging with the Infrastructure Odyssey narrative:

  • Supplement 1 (IDENTIFY) teaches you to recognize infrastructure patterns across history and nature
  • Supplement 2 (UNDERSTAND) helps you comprehend how these patterns interconnect and evolve
  • Supplement 3 (MANAGE & CONTROL) empowers you to apply this knowledge in practical scenarios
  • Supplement 4 (THESIS & VISION) challenges you to imagine and design future infrastructure systems
