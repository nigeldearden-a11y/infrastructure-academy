ABOUT THIS VOLUME

Volume 1: Perspective presents the narrative foundation of "An Infrastructure Odyssey." Through twelve chapters, each focused on a civilizational relay, this volume traces 12,000 years of human infrastructure development. Each chapter combines historical narrative with analysis of how infrastructure enabled or constrained civilizational progress.
